Now

I'm Murad — a Computer Engineering student at Gazi University in Ankara. Third year, GPA 3.50/4.00.

I've been building games since I can remember. Currently deep in a Terraria-style pixel platformer with procedural tilemap generation. Sleep Shipping things is the priority.

In 2025 my team won the Most Original Software Award at TEKNOFEST and placed 5th nationally for our 5G indoor positioning system — a weird combination of fingerprinting, multilateration, and PyTorch/XGBoost that somehow worked.

I chair the Digital Game Design Society at Gazi University. Previously board member of both the R&D team and the Data Science Society. Running communities is harder than it looks.

I work in Unity 6 and Godot 4, mostly C# and GDScript. Python for everything ML-related. Running Arch and Fedora — yes, both.

Three languages: Turkish (native), English (C1), Arabic (B1). Working on not mixing them mid-sentence.
